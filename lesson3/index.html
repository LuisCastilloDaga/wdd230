Site Name

The site name will be the Lima North Chamber. As the name indicates, this is a chamber of commerce page of the North of Lima City.

Purpose Statement

Our purpose is to evaluate, survey, and classify all the aspects of every type of product and service in our city. In order to bring a good advice to the people who are interested in investing in our town. Our service to the community is offered by conferences, articles in magazines, and direct advice.
